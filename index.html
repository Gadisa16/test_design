Frequently Asked Questions

Not sure how it works? We have compiled answers to the most common questions so that you can easily submit your complaint/feedback.

Do I have a complaint or feedback?

You can submit both. Choose the appropriate category on the submission form and provide details to help us process it efficiently.

Where do I select the language on complaint webpage?

Use the Languages option at the top-right corner of the page to change your preferred language.

What type of customer can I choose for registration?

Individuals and businesses are supported. Select the option that best describes you while registering.

How can I create a sub-distributor account?

After registering as a distributor, navigate to Account Settings → Team to invite sub-accounts and assign roles.

How do I activate my webpage account?

Click the activation link sent to your email after registration. If you didn’t receive it, use “Resend email” on the login page.
